# Product Design Handoff

Use when product-design concept work is ready to hand to serious design, Stitch production flow, frontend, or stakeholders.

Read first:
- `../product-design-common/references/product-design-contract.md`
- `../product-design-common/references/handoff-packet.md`
- `../product-design-common/references/output-shapes.md`
- `../product-design-common/references/safety-boundaries.md`

Workflow:
1. Gather the brief, opportunity, journey, page specs, review notes, and concept artifacts.
2. Separate source truth from generated concept evidence.
3. List open questions, risks, and recommended next bundle.
4. Produce a compact handoff packet.
5. Stop before mutating design repo or implementation files.

Output:
- handoff packet
- source-truth summary
- concept/prototype artifacts
- risks and open questions
- recommended downstream lane
- missing evidence
- assumptions
