# Product Design Handoff

Use when product-design concept work is ready to hand to serious design, Stitch production flow, frontend, or stakeholders.

Read first:
- `../product-design-common/references/product-design-contract.md`
- `../product-design-common/references/handoff-packet.md`
- `../product-design-common/references/output-shapes.md`
- `../product-design-common/references/e2e-artifact-contract.md`
- `../product-design-common/references/safety-boundaries.md`

Workflow:
1. Gather the brief, market wedge, opportunity, journey, page specs, review notes, images, UI concept, and promotion verdict.
2. Separate source truth from generated concept evidence.
3. List artifact inventory and pass/fail/degraded/blocked status.
4. List open questions, risks, missing evidence, and recommended next bundle.
5. Produce a compact handoff packet.
6. Stop before mutating design repo or implementation files.

Output:
- handoff packet
- source-truth summary
- concept/UI artifact inventory and status
- key decisions with evidence/standard basis
- risks and open questions
- recommended downstream lane
- next validation step
- missing evidence
- assumptions
