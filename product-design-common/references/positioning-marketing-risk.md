# Positioning and Marketing Risk

Check marketing risk only as it affects product/design concept quality.

Look for:
- unclear audience
- weak or generic value proposition
- misleading or unsupported claims
- feature promise beyond product reality
- trust/compliance gaps
- pricing or CTA confusion
- social proof/testimonial misuse
- conversion path mismatch

Do not create campaign plans, channel strategy, ad systems, launch calendars, or growth experiments here. Route those to a future marketing/GTM bundle.
