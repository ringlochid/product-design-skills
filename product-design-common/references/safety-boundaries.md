# Safety Boundaries

Allowed by default:
- local files in scope
- public web pages/docs/repos
- read-only analysis and concept writing
- local reference snapshots

Ask first:
- account cookies or logged-in sources
- private URLs/repos/customer data
- external writes, publishing, messaging, purchases, subscriptions
- creating/editing external Stitch projects if policy or account state is unclear

Do not store credentials, tokens, cookies, raw private URLs, or unnecessary PII in briefs, specs, prompts, or handoffs.

Generated prototypes can illustrate ideas but must not be presented as validated user research or market proof.
